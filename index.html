What is Disc Golf?

Disc Golf has a history dating back to the 1960s. The disc or frisbee is patented by Ed Headrick who is also known as the Father of Disc Golf. You throw the disc toward the Disc Golf Pole Hole. Scoring is tracked like golf, the smaller the number the better.

There is also an app for Apple or Google store where you can keep track of your scores on all sanctioned courses, called UDisc
